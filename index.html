Features

Most trackers only handle investments. This one handles your complete financial life — assets and debts together.

Complete net worth

Stocks, ETFs, crypto, real estate, cash, hybrid loans, and private equity — all tracked together with your debts for a true net worth figure.

Live prices

Real-time stock and ETF prices via Finnhub (free API key, no card needed) and crypto prices via CoinGecko. Always up to date.

Real analytics

CAGR, time-to-double projections, best and worst performer breakdowns, historical quarter comparisons. Know if your money is actually working.

Quarterly PDF reports

Automatic quarterly snapshots with PDF export. Track your performance quarter by quarter with a full breakdown of what moved your net worth.

CSV import

Import your portfolio via CSV from Robinhood, Nordnet, and Revolut. Get your existing positions loaded instantly without manual entry.

Privacy first

Everything stays on your device. Optional Google Drive backup. No account required, no data sent to any server — ever.
